Benefits of working with me

Where the manuals map the path, coaching walks it with you.

Every manual gives you the C.R.E.A.T.E. framework to work through on your own. Coaching is what happens when you want someone alongside you while you do it — applying the same framework to your actual circumstances, not just the page.

I've lived every step in every one of these manuals myself — not just written them.

Creative Journey

Where the manual asks you to explore what draws you, coaching helps you actually follow through on it — someone holding you to the excitement you found.

Meditation

Stillness is easy to read about and hard to sustain alone. Sessions give the practice a check-in that keeps it honest.

Breathwork

A technique only proves itself under real pressure, not on the page. Coaching is where we test it against your actual stress, together.

Better Habits

The manual gives you the steps. Sessions give you someone checking in on what's genuinely changing, and adjusting the approach when it isn't.

Wellness

Rest, food, and movement look different in theory than in a real week. Coaching adapts the foundations to the life you're actually living.

Slow Life

Doing less is harder than it sounds when everyone around you is doing more. Coaching gives you permission and structure to actually slow down.

Primal Fitness

Strength built alone can stall quietly. Sessions keep the momentum honest, without gym theatre or guilt.

Sobriety & Recovery

Work through the hardest modules with a Recovery Coach Professional who built this framework from twenty-five years of harmful habits, not theory.

Neurowellness & Craft

Making something with your hands is transformative. Coaching helps you find the version of that which fits your life, not just mine.

Grief & Loss

Some chapters aren't meant to be worked through alone. Coaching gives you room to go slower, or say something out loud before you write it down.

This isn't a substitute for medical, psychiatric, or therapeutic care. If you're in crisis, please contact your GP, the Samaritans, or emergency services first.
