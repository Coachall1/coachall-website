Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 2026 · Coachall, Brighton, UK

Coachall is run by Spencer Hall ("I", "me"). This notice explains what personal data I collect, why, and your rights over it. I keep this as short and plain as the manuals.

What I collect and why

  • Email signups (guides and letters). Your email address, so I can send the free guides and the short letters you asked for. Sent via MailerLite. Unsubscribe any time with one click — lawful basis: consent.
  • Purchases. Your name, email and payment details are handled by Payhip and its payment processors (Stripe/PayPal) to deliver your manuals and meet tax obligations. I never see your card details — lawful basis: contract.
  • Coaching enquiries and bookings. Your name, email and what you choose to share, via Calendly and email, so we can arrange and hold sessions — lawful basis: contract / legitimate interest.
  • Website analytics. If analytics are enabled, only privacy-friendly, cookie-less measurement is used (no advertising trackers, ever).

What I never do

I never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or add you to lists you didn't ask for. Anything you tell me in coaching stays confidential, within the limits of the law and safety.

Processors I use

MailerLite (email), Payhip (shop), Stripe/PayPal (payments), Calendly (bookings), GitHub Pages (hosting). Each processes data under its own terms and, where required, appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You can ask at any time to see, correct, export or delete your personal data, or withdraw consent for the letters. Email spencer@coachall.co.uk and I'll act within 30 days. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Retention

Emails stay on the list until you unsubscribe. Purchase records are kept as required for accounting (normally 6 years). Coaching notes, where kept, are deleted within 12 months of our work ending unless you ask otherwise.

Contact

Questions about this notice: spencer@coachall.co.uk.
